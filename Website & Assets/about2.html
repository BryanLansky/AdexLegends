Arena | Game Play
You will play as team in this game. There will be 30 teams in one maps. In this gaame, if you wat to be a champian, you guys should killed enemies and be the lasst man standing team. There will be 5 round, in every round ring fire will get smaller and smaller
Battle Royale | Game Play
Player will be dropped at choosen maps and will fight each other 3 vs 3 in 10 rounds. For win this game, your team should get higher poin than your enemies. If you get same point, there will be another rount to choose the winner.
